# outreach.md — LinkedIn and Cold Outreach Mode

**Trigger:** `/outreach [company name or person name]`

Read `modes/_shared.md` first.

---

## Step 1 — Identify the Target

Determine what Brian wants:
- Cold outreach to a recruiter at a target company
- Warm outreach to someone in Brian's network at a company
- LinkedIn connection request to a hiring manager
- Thank-you note after an interview

Ask if not specified: "Who are we reaching out to, and what's the goal?"

---

## Step 2 — Research the Person

Use WebSearch to find:
- Their name and current role
- Their LinkedIn (if publicly visible)
- Any shared background (AWS re/Start, Charlotte, security community)
- Recent posts or activity that can serve as a hook

---

## Step 3 — Draft Outreach Message

### LinkedIn Connection Request (300 char limit)
```
"Hi [Name] — I'm a cloud/security practitioner transitioning from ops.
I saw [specific thing: their post / company's job posting / shared community].
Would love to connect and learn from your path into [their field]."
```

### Cold InMail / Email (targeted)
```
Subject: [Role Title] at [Company] — Referral Request or Quick Question

Hi [Name],

I'm [Brian] — AWS re/Start grad, Azure SOC practitioner, currently pursuing CLF-C02 and CySA+.
I applied for the [role] at [company] on [date].

[One sentence about why you're reaching out to THEM specifically — their team, their background, their post.]

Would you be open to a 15-minute call? Happy to work around your schedule.

[Brian's name + LinkedIn URL]
```

### Post-Interview Thank You
```
Subject: Thank you — [Role] interview

Hi [Name],

Thanks for the time today. I especially enjoyed talking about [specific topic from interview].

It reinforced that [Company]'s work on [specific thing] is exactly the direction I'm heading.

Looking forward to next steps.

[Brian]
```

---

## Outreach Rules

1. Reference something specific — generic messages get ignored
2. Short > long — under 100 words for cold outreach
3. Don't ask for a job — ask for a conversation or connection
4. Never send to more than 3 people at the same company simultaneously
5. Log all outreach in `data/applications.md` Notes field
6. NEVER send without Brian's review

---

## Step 4 — Save Outreach Log

Append to the Notes field in `data/applications.md` for the relevant company:
`Outreach sent to [Name] on [date] via [LinkedIn/Email]`
