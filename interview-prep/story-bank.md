# STAR Story Bank

Stories for behavioral interview questions. Add new stories using `/interview` mode
or directly below. Each story maps to one or more competencies.

---

## Template

```
### Story: [Short title]
**Competency:** [e.g., problem-solving, threat detection, leadership, cross-team collaboration]
**Best used for:** [e.g., SOC Analyst, Cloud Security roles]

**Situation:** [Context — what was happening, what was at stake]

**Task:** [What you specifically needed to do]

**Action:** [What you did, step by step — be specific about tools and decisions]

**Result:** [Quantifiable outcome or clear impact — what changed because of your actions]

**Key phrase:** [One memorable sentence that captures the story — use this as your closer]
```
